About ePetCare

Our Story

ePetCare is an innovative web-based system designed to streamline veterinary clinic operations and enhance pet healthcare management. Our platform focuses on supporting small-scale veterinary practices by integrating essential features such as digital pet health records, appointment scheduling, inventory monitoring, and client communication tools — all in one accessible system.

We aim to empower veterinarians with a reliable management solution that minimizes manual work, reduces paperwork, and ensures accurate and organized recordkeeping. At the same time, pet owners can conveniently access their pets' medical histories, vaccination schedules, and upcoming appointments through an intuitive interface.

Our Mission

At ePetCare, our mission is to simplify clinic management while improving the overall quality of pet healthcare. By embracing technology, we help veterinarians focus more on what truly matters — caring for animals.

Digital Health Records
